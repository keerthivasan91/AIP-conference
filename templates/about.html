About UTCA 2026

It gives us immense pleasure to invite you to join UTCA-2026, International Conference on Ubiquitous Technology in Communication & Artificial Intelligence- 2026 (UTCA-2026), to be held at Cambridge Institute of Technology, K R Puram Bangalore. The conference is being organized with an aim to provide a platform for researchers, scientists, technocrats, academicians and engineers to exchange their innovative ideas. This Conference will be a platform to focus on the core technological developments in the emerging fields like Wireless communication, RF microwave/Radar, Image computing, intelligent information retrieval, data mining, artificial intelligence, optimization, machine learning methods/ algorithms, evolutionary computing and so on. UTCA-2026 is premier interdisciplinary platform for leading academic scientists, researchers and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results on all aspects of communication system.
