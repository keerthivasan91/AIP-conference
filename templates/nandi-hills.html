Nandi Hills

Nandi Hills is a scenic hill station located near Bengaluru, famous for its breathtaking sunrise views, cool climate, and lush greenery. Situated at an elevation of about 1,478 meters above sea level, Nandi Hills has historical significance and offers a peaceful retreat from the city. It is a popular destination for nature lovers, photographers, and adventure enthusiasts visiting Bengaluru.
