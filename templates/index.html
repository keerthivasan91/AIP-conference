About
Cambridge Institute of Technology, Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Founded in 2007 by the Umesh Education Trust, the Cambridge Institute of Technology (CIT), Bengaluru, is an autonomous institution affiliated with VTU, distinguished by its NAAC A+ and NBA accreditations. Located on a 10-acre campus in K.R. Puram, the institute offers a comprehensive academic portfolio including eight B.E. programs, M.B.A., M.C.A., and various M.Tech and Ph.D. tracks supported by eight research centers. CIT is recognized as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO) and earned a spot in the 101–150 band of the 2023 NIRF Innovation Rankings. Its infrastructure is notably advanced, featuring industry-sponsored laboratories and a specialized metallic 3D printing facility. The academic environment is driven by a faculty body composed of experts from IITs, NITs, and prestigious international universities. This expertise, combined with a robust placement ecosystem involving over 200 companies like TCS, Rakuten, and HSBC, ensures high industry readiness for its students. Beyond academics, CIT fosters entrepreneurship through its MSME incubation center and a dedicated Section 8 company for research and innovation, maintaining a global legacy through its 10,000+ successful alumni.
