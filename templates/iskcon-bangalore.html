ISKCON Temple, Bangalore

ISKCON Temple Bangalore is one of the largest and most prominent Krishna temples in the world. Dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha, the temple is renowned for its grand architecture, spiritual atmosphere, and cultural significance. Located atop Hare Krishna Hill, it offers visitors a serene experience combining devotion, art, and panoramic views of the city.
