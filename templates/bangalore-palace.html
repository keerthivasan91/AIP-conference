Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace is a magnificent heritage landmark that reflects the royal legacy of the Mysore dynasty. Built in 1878, the palace features Tudor-style architecture inspired by England’s Windsor Castle, with ornate wood carvings, grand halls, and beautifully maintained gardens. It offers visitors a glimpse into Bengaluru’s regal past and cultural heritage.
