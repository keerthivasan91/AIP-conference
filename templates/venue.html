Venue

Cambridge Institute of Technology, Bangalore, Karnataka, India

Cambridge Institute of Technology (CIT), Bengaluru, established in 2007 by the Umesh Education Trust, is an autonomous institution affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi. Located at K.R. Puram, the institute offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across engineering, management, and research disciplines. CIT is accredited with NAAC A+, holds NBA accreditation for several programs, and is recognized as a SIRO by the Government of India. Getting There

By Air

Kempegowda International Airport (BLR) is about 35 km from the campus.

By Train

Krishnarajapuram (KR Puram) Railway Station is the nearest station, located around 5 km from the institute. | KSR Bangalore City Junction Railway Station is about 18 km away.

By Road

The campus is well connected with NH 75 and is easily accessible by bus, taxi, or auto-rickshaw from various parts of Bangalore.
